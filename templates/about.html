Booksters is a comunity of books fans that would like to get together and share their experiences about books.

In Booksters you can add books that you like and rate or comment on books shared in the comunity.

In Booksters we trust each other. For this reason, we are giving you the possiblity to edit or even delete all books. Please, remember you are suppose to delete or edit a book only if you have created it.
